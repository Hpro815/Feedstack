Privacy Policy

01

Who We Are

Feedstack is a habit-building app that lets you attach media links from YouTube, X, and other sources to daily reminders so you can turn saved content into real habits. Feedstack is an independent app developed by a solo founder.

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at privacy@feedstack.app.

02

What Data We Collect

Feedstack is designed to collect as little data as possible. Here is exactly what we collect and why:

  • Habit data — the names, links, reminder times, and completion history you create in the app. This is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and is never sent to our servers.
  • Link metadata — when you paste a URL, the app fetches publicly available metadata (title, thumbnail) from that URL to show you a preview. We do not store this request on our servers.
  • Notification preferences — the times you choose to be reminded. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Crash reports (anonymous) — if the app crashes, we may receive an anonymous error report with no personal information attached. This helps us fix bugs.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal identifiers unless you voluntarily provide them when contacting support.

03

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to make the app work for you:

  • To display your habits on your daily dashboard
  • To send you local push notifications at your chosen times
  • To calculate and display your habit streaks
  • To fetch link previews when you paste a URL
  • To fix bugs and improve app stability via anonymous crash reports

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for commercial purposes. Full stop.

The only external services the app interacts with are:

  • YouTube oEmbed API — used to fetch video titles and thumbnails when you paste a YouTube link. This is a public API that does not require an account or send any personal data.
  • Open Graph / website metadata — used to fetch previews for non-YouTube links. A standard web request, no personal data transmitted.
  • Expo / EAS (crash reporting) — anonymous crash reports only, with no personally identifiable information.
05

Push Notifications

Feedstack uses local push notifications only. This means your reminders are scheduled entirely on your device — they are not sent from a server and do not require an internet connection to fire.

We will never send you marketing notifications. The only notifications you will receive are the ones you set up yourself for your own habits.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings or within the app.

06

Data Retention & Deletion

Because your habit data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it at all times.

  • You can delete individual habits from within the app at any time
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling Feedstack from your device
  • If you contact us for support and share information via email, we will delete that correspondence within 90 days upon request
07

Children's Privacy

Feedstack is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

08

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are in the European Economic Area or California, you have additional rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — you can request that we delete any data we hold about you
  • Right to portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — you can object to certain processing of your data

Given that Feedstack stores data locally on your device and collects minimal information, most of these rights are exercisable directly within the app. For any additional requests, contact us at privacy@feedstack.app.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are significant, we will notify you via an in-app message.

Your continued use of Feedstack after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
